Our Story

I first saw Angela in the autumn of 2020 when I joined Ericsson as a graduate. While we met virtually through work, I slowly grew fond of her. What started as a professional relationship gradually blossomed into a close friendship. We played video games online, talked for hours over calls, and watched movies together. Finally, in 2021, after almost a year, I made the big decision to move to Melbourne for work. When we met in person, a fire sparked between us, and the rest is history.
