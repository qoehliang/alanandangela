Travel

Getting to Yering Station

Yering Station is located at 38 Melba Hwy, Yering VIC 3770. It's about an hour's drive from Melbourne CBD.

  • By Car: Follow the Eastern Freeway (M3) and then take the Maroondah Highway (B360). There is ample parking available at the venue.
  • By Taxi/Ride Share: You can easily book a taxi or rideshare (Uber/Ola) to Yering Station. Please allow extra time for travel, especially during peak hours.
  • Wedding Bus: For your convenience, a bus will be provided for guests travelling from Melbourne CBD.
    • Pickup Location: Russell Street Extension (rear of Federation Square)
    • Departure Time: 2:30 PM
    • Return Time: 11:15 PM

Accommodation

Traveling from afar? We recommend these Melbourne CBD hotels, with charter bus service to and from the city:

  • Adelphi: 5-star boutique hotel with rooftop pool and city views.
  • YEHS Melbourne Hotel CBD: Budget-friendly option with simple rooms, some with kitchens, and a cafe.

Driving and staying the night before? Consider this option near the venue:

  • Chateau Yering Hotel: Contact us for a discount!

You can also find other options on Booking.com or Airbnb.
